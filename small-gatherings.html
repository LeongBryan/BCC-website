Our Care Groups


At BCC, we believe that we grow as Christians as we hear the word clearly and faithfully taught. Hence, besides our big gatherings, we have smaller gatherings or Care groups where we’re organised into our various age groups so that we can learn and apply God’s Word to our particular life situation and context.
