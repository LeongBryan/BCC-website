READ@W11 | LEARN @W11


READ@W11 and LEARN@W11 are initiatives as part of Project: Love Your Neighbour (LYN), where we organise literacy and tuition programs respectively for children and teenagers in the community. We also organise special events such as cooking workshops, and trips to the Singapore Zoo!


READ

READ@W11 is a literacy programme that supports children aged 4 to 9 years old, who are facing literacy challenges. The programme offers a phonics-based curriculum, building their confidence and skill in reading the alphabet and simple words. Our teachers engage our students through games, storytelling sessions, songs and craft.

Time: 10am-12pm, Saturdays
Location: 11 Woodlands Close #03-39 | 737853

LEARN

LEARN@W11 is a tuition and supervised learning programme for children aged 10 to 12 years old. LEARN@W11 offers academic support to primary school students in the mathematics and science subjects.
